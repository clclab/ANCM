Welcome to ANCM’22#

This website contains the lab assignments for the Advanced Neural and Cognitive Modelling course. You can access the assignments through the menu on the left.

To open a lab assignment in google colab, go to the assignment page and hover your mouse over the rocket symbol ( ) in the top right, then choose Colab. Once on colab, don’t forget to make a copy of the notebook in your own Drive to save your changes. If you prefer working in your own local jupyter environment, you can also download ( ) the .ipynb file for any lab assignment.
